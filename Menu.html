Explore Your Taste Buds with Rice-Burst Bites
Flavour that Packs a Punch


  1. Classic
    1. Salmon: Grilled salmon flakes inside a rice ball, wrapped with seaweed.
    2. Tuna Mayo: Canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise and soy sauce.
    3. Umeboshi: Pickled plum (umeboshi) filling for a tangy kick.


  2. Vegetarian/ Vegan Option
    • Shiitake Mushroom: Marinated shiitake mushrooms with soy sauce.
    • Avocado and Cucumber: Fresh avocado and cucumber for a refreshing bite.
    • Sesame & Salt: Simple yet flavorful with sesame seeds and a pinch of salt.


  3. Fusion and Unique Flavors
    • Spicy Tuna: Tuna mixed with spicy mayo, adding a spicy-sweet flavor.
    • Teriyaki Chicken: Chicken cooked in teriyaki sauce as a savory filling.
    • Kimchi: Spicy Korean kimchi mixed into the rice for a tangy and spicy twist.


  4. Grilled
    • Soy Sauce: Rice ball brushed with soy sauce and grilled until crispy.
    • Miso Butter: A mix of miso and butter spread on the rice and grilled for a rich flavor.


  5. Sweet
    1. Sweet Red Bean: Sweetened red bean paste as a filling, for a dessert-like option.
    2. Coconut and Mango: Rice filled with diced mango, rolled in coconut flakes.
